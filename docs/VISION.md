# Vision

## Why Home Energy Advisor Exists

Modern homes generate more energy data than ever before.

Home Assistant provides an excellent foundation for collecting and displaying that data, but understanding the financial impact of everyday energy usage remains difficult. Users can often see where energy comes from and how much has been consumed, yet answering simple questions such as *"What did my pool pump actually cost me today?"* or *"How much money did my solar panels save by running the dishwasher this afternoon?"* still requires manual calculation or educated guesswork.

Home Energy Advisor exists to bridge that gap.

Its purpose is not simply to present more information, but to transform energy data into knowledge that helps people make better decisions.

## Vision

Home Energy Advisor helps people understand the financial impact of how they use energy in their homes.

By combining energy consumption, local generation and energy pricing, the product provides meaningful insights into what devices cost to run, where money is being spent and where opportunities exist to reduce costs without compromising comfort.

Over time, the product should evolve from explaining what has happened, to helping users understand why it happened, and ultimately to supporting better decisions about future energy usage.

## Design Philosophy

Every feature should answer at least one meaningful question that a user could not easily answer before.

The product should favour clarity over complexity, practical value over technical sophistication and incremental improvements over large, infrequent releases.

Features are added because they improve decisions, not because they are technically interesting.

## Target Users

The initial focus is Home Assistant users who:

* monitor their household energy usage;
* generate energy through solar or other local sources;
* use dynamic or time-of-use electricity tariffs; or
* want to better understand the financial impact of running individual devices.

The product should remain accessible to users with simple installations while scaling naturally to more advanced energy systems.

## Long-Term Direction

The long-term goal is to become a trusted companion for understanding household energy usage.

Insights, recommendations and future automation should build upon a solid foundation of accurate accounting and transparent calculations.

Users should always understand why a recommendation has been made and remain in control of how the product is used.

## Out of Scope

Home Energy Advisor is not intended to replace Home Assistant's Energy Dashboard.

It complements the existing ecosystem by providing financial insight and decision support built on top of the data Home Assistant already collects.

Similarly, the product should avoid becoming a general-purpose home automation platform. Automation may be enabled where it provides clear value, but understanding and trust should always come before autonomous behaviour.
